French Toast

Home

Description

This fluffy French toast recipe is crisp on the outside, but perfectly soft and tender on the inside. Top this French toast with maple syrup, powdered sugar, and fresh fruit (such as sliced strawberries and bananas) for a beautiful and delicious presentation

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinammon
  • 1 teaspoon whtie sugar
  • 1½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 12 slices of bread

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Place flour into a large mixing bowl; slowly whisk in milk until smooth. Whisk in eggs, sugar, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt until well combined.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat. Meanwhile, soak bread slices in milk mixture until saturated.
  4. Working in batches, cook bread on the preheated griddle or pan until golden brown on both sides, about 2 minutes per side.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy.
